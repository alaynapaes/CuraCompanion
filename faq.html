Frequently Asked Questions

What is Cura Companion?
Cura Companion is a digital platform designed to support caregivers with resources, mental health tools, and interactive guidance.
Who can use Cura Companion?
It’s built for family caregivers, healthcare aides, and anyone providing long-term care to loved ones or patients.
Is Cura Companion free to use?
Yes! The basic version is free, offering access to caregiver tips, stress management tools, and daily guidance.
Do I need any technical knowledge to use it?
Not at all. The platform is designed to be simple and intuitive, even for those with little or no digital experience.
Does Cura Companion provide emergency help?
While Cura Companion offers guidance and mental health support, it does not replace emergency medical services. Always contact local emergency numbers in urgent situations.
Can I access Cura Companion on mobile devices?
Yes, it’s fully responsive and works on smartphones, tablets, and desktops.
Is my personal data safe?
Absolutely. We follow strict privacy protocols to ensure that your data remains secure and confidential at all times.
How can I contact the support team?
You can email us at support@curacompanion.com or use the “Contact Us” form on our website.
